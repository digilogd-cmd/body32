# BODY32 Guardian Art Direction — Representative Style Sheet v1

## Decision

The first production reference uses four representative Guardians: Ignite Tiger, Weave Fox, Ground Bear, and Reflect Owl. They test all four Rhythm materials and four distinct silhouette families before production scales to all 32 types.

## Locked qualities

- Shared simplified anatomy, proportions, eye language, lighting, and gallery presentation
- Calm, alert expressions without infantile or aggressive mascot cues
- Rounded and tapered geometry with softly dimensional surfaces
- Warm ceramic for Ignite, soft glass for Weave, matte clay/stone for Ground, moonlit porcelain for Reflect
- Clear silhouette recognition at avatar size
- No clothing, props, gender coding, national symbolism, sacred motifs, or medical claims

## Reference asset

`assets/characters/concepts/guardian-style-sheet-v1.png`

This is an internal art-direction reference, not a final runtime character crop. Final assets require individual pose review, transparent master production, accessibility descriptions, and cultural QA.
